## Course Curriculum

### Module 1: Situational Awareness Fundamentals
- Understanding the OODA loop (Observe, Orient, Decide, Act)
- Recognizing normal vs. abnormal behavior
- Environmental scanning techniques
- Pre-attack indicators to watch for
- Maintaining awareness in different settings

### Module 2: Self-Defense Techniques
- Basic strikes (palm heel, elbow, knee)
- Escaping wrist grabs and bear hugs
- Ground defense basics
- Using leverage and body mechanics
- Practice drills and muscle memory

### Module 3: Personal Safety Tools & Technology
- Safety apps: features, limitations, and best practices
- Personal alarms: types and usage
- Understanding pepper spray and alternatives
- Legal considerations and restrictions
- Building and maintaining your safety kit

### Module 4: Protective Strategies for Daily Life
- Safe parking and vehicle security
- Walking and jogging safety protocols
- Public transportation awareness
- Social situations and boundaries
- Building and maintaining a safety network

### Module 5: Mental Preparedness & Confidence
- Overcoming fear and building confidence
- Decision-making under pressure
- Creating safety plans for common scenarios
- Building sustainable safety habits
- Supporting others in your community
