## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: What courses do you offer?**
A: We offer comprehensive courses on online privacy & security, public safety & awareness, and travel safety. All courses are designed specifically for women.

**Q: Are the courses free?**
A: Many of our educational resources are free. Some advanced courses may have fees, but we offer scholarships for those in need. Contact us to learn more.

**Q: How can I get involved?**
A: There are many ways to get involved! You can volunteer, donate, advocate for privacy rights, or help spread awareness. Contact us to discuss opportunities.

### Course Questions

**Q: Do I need prior experience?**
A: No! Our courses are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to those with more experience.

**Q: Are courses available online?**
A: Yes, many of our courses are available online. We also offer in-person workshops in select locations.

**Q: Can I access course materials after completion?**
A: Yes, course participants receive ongoing access to materials and resources.

### Privacy & Safety Questions

**Q: Is my information safe when I contact you?**
A: Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and protect all personal information in accordance with our privacy policy.

**Q: Can you help me with an urgent safety concern?**
A: For immediate safety concerns, please contact local authorities (911) or appropriate emergency services. We can provide resources and support, but cannot replace emergency services.

### Advocacy Questions

**Q: How can I help advocate for privacy rights?**
A: Visit our [Digital Advocacy](/digital-advocacy) page to learn about ways to get involved, contact your representatives, and support privacy legislation.

**Q: Do you work with other organizations?**
A: Yes, we partner with various organizations and advocacy groups. Contact our partnerships team to discuss collaboration.

## Emergency Resources

**If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services immediately:**

- **Emergency (U.S.)**: 911
- **National Domestic Violence Hotline**: 1-800-799-7233
- **National Sexual Assault Hotline**: 1-800-656-4673
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741

For non-emergency safety concerns or questions about our programs, please use our contact form or email support@encrypther.org.
