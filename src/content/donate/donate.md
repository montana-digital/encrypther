## Your Impact

Every donation directly supports women's safety education and empowerment. Here's how your contribution helps:

### Free Educational Courses
- **Online Privacy & Security**: Help women learn to protect their digital footprint and remove their data from broker sites
- **Public Safety & Awareness**: Provide situational awareness and self-defense training that can reduce violence risk by up to 50%
- **Travel Safety**: Teach women to detect hidden cameras, identify tracking devices, and travel safely

### Privacy Advocacy
- Support our work fighting for comprehensive federal privacy legislation
- Help us contact representatives and advocate for digital rights
- Fund research and educational resources about privacy protection
- Build a movement for privacy rights for all Americans

### Community Building
- Create a supportive network of empowered women
- Provide resources to those who need them most
- Fund community events and workshops
- Support outreach to underserved communities

We believe in complete transparency about how your donations are used:
