## The Travel Safety Challenge

✈️ **1 in 4 female travelers say they've felt unsafe or threatened when traveling alone.** Whether for business or pleasure, traveling should be an empowering experience, not a source of fear.

From hidden cameras in hotel rooms to tracking devices on rental cars, modern travelers face unique security challenges. Our workshops teach women how to identify danger before it starts — from rideshare red flags to hotel security checks.

## What You'll Learn

This comprehensive course covers essential travel safety and privacy protection:

### Hidden Camera Detection
- Common hiding spots for hidden cameras
- Detection tools and techniques
- What to do if you find a hidden camera
- Legal considerations and reporting
- Protecting yourself in accommodations

### Tracking Device Detection
- Types of tracking devices (GPS, RFID, Bluetooth)
- How to scan for tracking devices
- Where trackers are commonly placed
- What to do if you find a tracker
- Protecting your vehicle and belongings

### Travel Privacy Protection
- Secure communication while traveling
- Protecting digital devices from theft and hacking
- Safe use of public Wi-Fi
- Protecting your personal information
- Financial security while traveling

### Pre-Travel Security Planning
- Researching destinations and accommodations
- Safe booking practices
- Creating a travel safety plan
- Emergency contacts and resources
- Travel insurance considerations
